The Impossible Line of the Horizon

for The Orchestra of the League of Composers

The mood of this music is contemplative, as if the floating, suspended time it suggests depicts an ongoing sunset in which clouds and different hues appear over an infinite horizon.

Performed by the Orchestra of the League of Composers (leagueofcomposers.org), at the NYU FAS Graduate Composer's Concert, Merkin Hall, on May 5th 2014

La Línea Imposible del Horizonte

paraThe Orchestra of the League of Composers

Esta música apela a la contemplación: el tiempo suspendido y flotante que convoca sugiere un atardecer permanente, en el cual aparecen diferentes nubes y tonalidades, sobre un horizonte infinito.

Estrenado por The Orchestra of the League of Composers (leagueofcomposers.org), durante el concierto de los estudiantes de posgrado de NYU, FAS, en la sala Merkin Hall, el 5 de Mayo de 2014
