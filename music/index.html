Untitled (6 channel mixdown)

"Untitled" is a question regarding creativity, unpredictability and mechanical determinism. The audio consists of random and chaos generators in real-time synthesis. The video contains images of turbulence taken from a river in Cordoba (Argentina), cellular automata noise, and randomized nouns. The score has ordered sections made out of randomized variables, and the last section requires some human action. Sounds and images were made with Pure Data for the Immersion Lab at NYU Bobst Library.

  • https://soundcloud.com/ffddcchh/untitled-6ch-mix
    •

Premiered by Fede Camara Halac on Thu Aug 03 2017, at Virginia Tech Cube Fest 2017
